About Zane Cuxton

Zane Cuxton is a globally recognized behavioural trainer, transformational mentor, and pioneering face reading expert with over four decades of experience across corporate leadership, personal development, and educational training.

Originally trained in the pharmaceutical industry, Zane began his professional journey in sales and marketing, culminating in a 12-year tenure as Vice President – Marketing. A postgraduate in Sales, and one of the few ISO-certified trainers, he brings a unique blend of Western business acumen and Eastern wisdom to his work.

A pivotal life transition in 1993 led Zane to embrace his passion for human behaviour, non-verbal communication, and face reading—a journey shaped by personal transformation and decades of observation and study. This marked the beginning of his extraordinary career in human resource development and behavioural sciences.

Founder & Trailblazer
