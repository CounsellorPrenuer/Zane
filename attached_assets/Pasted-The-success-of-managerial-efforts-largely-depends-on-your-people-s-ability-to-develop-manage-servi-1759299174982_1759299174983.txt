Insights into Programs

Assertiveness
This programme is a must for every individual. In today’s world It is not good for the psychological well being of an individual, who follows either an aggressive or passive behavior. This programme deals in depth on ways and means using which an individual could improve his/her self-esteem, diplomatically ask for what they want, say No, handle criticism, give feedback and basically become more assertive in his/her personal or professional life.   
Programme Contents
Assertiveness and integrity, Keeping a balance, Three behavior types, Rights & Responsibilities, Assertive activities
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)
Communication
To be an effective communicator, one must uncover and transcend the barriers to communication within oneself and understand the complex process of interpersonal interaction. The programme will help participants develop a deeper understand of themselves and their relationships with others, cultivate proficiency in the art of dialogue, skills in persuasion, a communication style that stimulates achievement in others, ability to conduct fruitful meetings and conferences, and the confidence to be assertive in interacting with others.  
Programme Contents
Communication the challenge, The assumptions we make, Communication dynamics, Humour, Emotion, Staying in control
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)


Customer Service
This program provides an early opportunity to the Indian corporate world to appreciate customer service as the most powerful factor of corporate success. The objectives are to explain the concept of customer service by analyzing the benchmarks of successful customer service strategy and enrich the participants with the latest techniques, processes and skills so as to gain a competitive advantage.   
Programme Contents
Why should service matter, What is different about good service, Performance standards, Listening to customers, Assertiveness and Good Service, Effective Customer Communication, Internal Customers, Personal Action Planning, Self-Assessment Materials
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)

Dealing with problem behavior at the workplace
Most of us have by now come across people with problem behavior at the workplace. These people come in the disguise of a superior, college, subordinate, vendor, and customer, in fact in all possible disguises. Encounter with these indivuals leaves us psychologically shattered. More often than not these memories live on causing great emotional pain and ensure that we do not give our best to the jobs we do in future. No one has to live with this. In extreme cases lot of unwanted damage is done to the corporate by way of publicity in the media and yes even in the eyes of the law and the general public. This workshop helps in identifying the cause of such behavior and dealing with it in an efficient and diplomatic way. This would go along way in nipping the problems at the bud.
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)
Negotiation Skills
Getting that little extra! Handling price pressure more effectively calls for a higher level of competence and understanding of the process of negotiation. This program gives insights into the negotiation process, negotiation checklist and the relationship between price and profitability.  
Programme Contents
The core of the process, variables, power to influence, bridges of rapport, managing the process, interpersonal behavior, behavioral ‘ploys’, behavioral responses, principles of negotiations, tactics of negotiation.




Selling Skills
Demanding customer tougher market situations fall for an agile, competent, customer-driven sales team. This workshop would help the sales person to develop a result-oriented process, which would contribute to more successful sales, while all along keeping the customer in mind. In fact it would help the sales person to develop the ability to be a business partner to the customer rather than just a sales person.
Programme Contents
Pre-sale Preparation, Prospecting, Customer research, Objective and elements of good opening, Developing Need, Identifying buying criteria, Channeling buying criteria, Presenting a solution, Demonstration Skills, Handling Influencers, Objection handling, Negotiation, Buying signals, Post sales follow up
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)

Sales Closing Techniques
A lot of money is spent on sales and marketing in spite of which most of us still are not happy with the number of the sales closes. The reason why most sales men are not very productive is that they only concentrate on one type of a close. During this program we train the participants about different types of closes and their usage, thereby ensuring higher productivity.  

Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)
Team Building
One of the fundamental realities of organisational life is that people work in teams.  The success of managerial efforts therefore largely depends on the executive’s ability to develop and sustain the spirit of working in productive teams, be he a team member or team leader.  This program helps the participants to manage their own styles, which influence their relationship with others while all along developing skills of working with others and managing teams.
Programme Contents
Team definition, types of teams, team Vs groups, team buildings Vs team working, Six key areas of difficulty that teams can come up against, team objectives and individual responsibilities, Leadership style, leadership qualities.
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)
Telephone Handling Techniques
The telephone is the first link in almost all business communication. The way a caller is treated can either build a relationship or break a relationship. It is of utmost importance that those handling telephones are well equipped to give the caller a pleasant experience. 
Programme Contents
Effective telephone communication, Giving your callers a bad or good experience, Effective answering, Talking messages and transferring calls, Handling complaints, Preparing for and making effective calls, Getting what you want, Tele-marketing skills, Telephone Negotiation skills, Ways to build an use rapport
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)

The Winning Attitudes
This programme ensures that the trainee walks out after the program, more confident after discovering all the skills and talents, within him, this changes most mindsets. This programme also goes a long way in developing inter personal relationships, by reducing chances of common day to day misunderstandings.  Effective leadership is born during the workshop. 
Programme Contents
Developing communication, Listening, Attitude awareness, Dealing with emotions, Understanding people, Creative Problem-solving, Motivational power, Reaching your potential
Duration: 12 hrs (2 Days)
